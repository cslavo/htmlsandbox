Regex Cheatsheet

Character Classes

\d
Any digit (0-9)
\D
Any non-digit
\w
Word character (a-z, A-Z, 0-9, _)
\W
Non-word character
\s
Whitespace (space, tab, newline)
\S
Non-whitespace
[abc]
Any of a, b, or c
[^abc]
Not a, b, or c
[a-z]
Character range a to z
.
Any character except newline

Quantifiers

*
0 or more
+
1 or more
?
0 or 1 (optional)
{n}
Exactly n times
{n,}
n or more times
{n,m}
Between n and m times

Anchors

^
Start of string/line
$
End of string/line
\b
Word boundary
\B
Not word boundary

Groups & Lookaround

(abc)
Capturing group
(?:abc)
Non-capturing group
(?<name>abc)
Named group
(?=...)
Positive lookahead
(?!...)
Negative lookahead
